Equestrian Assisted Learning


We offer equestrian assisted growth and learning (EAL) sessions and workshops in a group setting or private one to one sessions.

The benefits of EAL are numerous and include:
  1. Increased self-awareness
  2. Increased awareness of non-verbal communication
  3. Improved confidence and assertiveness
  4. Team building and self effacy
